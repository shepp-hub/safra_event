Next meeting

Our eleventh meeting will take place on Thursday, Janurary 26, 2023, in the Jaglom Auditorium, Senate building, Tel Aviv University.
The talk is titled "Full-Field Cell Morphology - How computational photography and AI are changing diagnostics"
featuring Erez Naaman, Co-Founder & CTO at Scopio Labs.

In this talk, we will discuss the path that Scopio took in its journey to change the way hematology testing is done. We will dive into the technological gap that existed in imaging and the way we solved it, the dilemma between being a microscopy company or a diagnostics company and the challenges of creating a healthcare startup.
